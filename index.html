Bake Larne

Bake is a local family run business with two locations in the town, one in Craigyhill and one in the town centre. We specialise in range of fresh quality breads, sausage rolls, pies, pastries, cakes and scones.

We have two locations in Larne. Bake Craigyhill and Bake Broadway. You can find out the address and contact details about these pages from the Contact Us page.
